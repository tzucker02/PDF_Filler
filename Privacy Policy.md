# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the PDF Filler app (“the App”) handles user information.

## No data collection
The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or usage data from users.
This includes:

## No names, email addresses, or identifiers.
No device information, location, or IP‑address logging.
No cookies, analytics, tracking scripts, or third‑party monitoring tools.
Because the App collects no data, there is nothing to share, sell, or otherwise disclose to third parties.

If this ever changes in the future, an updated Privacy Policy will be published in the App or on its website, and users will be notified of any material changes.
